SCAD Course Selection Redesign

As a UI/UX designer, I worked with a UI designer to complete this project to improve the students’ experience when they tried to finish their degrees. I hope it helpful for them to find jobs in the future.

  • Role
  • UI/UX Designer
  • Sort
  • Team Project
  • Date
  • 3/2021
  • Duration
  • 2 Months
  • Problem
  • No Reference

Students are always hard to make choices among plenty of courses with a lot of elements such as time, position and skills required, especially when they want to take optional courses out of their majors.

  • Solution
  • Course Recommend System

We create tag system and course recommend system into the original system, which is helpful for the system to compute the plan list. Students can choose the best choice provided or make their personal choice.
