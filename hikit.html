Mobile App, UI/UX Design
HIKIT

HIKIT is the combination of application and accessories to help the people who get hurt and don’t have professonal medical care skills to do emergency treatment and get out of trouble.

  • Role
  • UI/UX Designer
  • Sort
  • Team Project
  • Date
  • 9/2020
  • Duration
  • 2 Months
  • Problem
  • Lack of emergency handling skills and first aid knowledge

When people are in the wild, in the face of emergencies (such as after a serious injury), they lack certain first aid capabilities. For the inexperienced, before going on a field trip, don't know what to bring just in case.

  • Solution
  • App & First Aid Equipment

We made an app for people to quickly find references to deal with in emergencies in the wild. At the same time, it is also equipped with the function of finding a nearby hospital, in case people are faced with a serious situation that they cannot handle. The app comes with a first aid kit, which is portable and has a wide variety of first aid kits.

Disadvantages

Competitive Analysis
Gold First-aid
Advantages
  • The app offers the function of voice assistant to call for a help.
  • The app offers a list of nearby hospitals for user to ask for a help.
  • It contains a list of first-aid articles.
Disadvantages
  • The quality of layout is poor
  • There is no content of accidents self-help methods in the app.
Competitive Analysis

Advantages
