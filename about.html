Hi, I am Runhao Chao. You can call me Ryan Chao!

I will graduate from The Savannah College of Art and Design in the fall of 2022 with an MFA degree in interaction design.

As a designer, my project experience tells me that all designs are iterative designs. For every project, we should stand on the user's point of view, think, do, and reflect.

For me, I prefer UX design. I read articles every day. Some are about design, some are about psychology, and some are other things. I want to avoid blind spots by knowing more widely. I think a designer is not just a designer, but a thinker, thinking about how to establish connections between things.
